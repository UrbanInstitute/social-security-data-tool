The average annual death-rate reduction is the average annual geometric rate of decline in the age-sex-adjusted death rate between 2013 and 2088. The overall age-sex-adjusted death rate decreases from 2013 to 2088 by 27 percent, 45 percent, and 60 percent for alternatives I, II, and III, respectively.
The average annual death-rate reductions used for this analysis are: 0.41 percent from the alternative I assumptions, 0.79 percent from the alternative II assumptions, and 1.20 percent from the alternative III assumptions. All other assumptions used for this analysis are from alternative II.
