The average annual death-rate reduction is the average annual geometric rate of decline in the age-sex- adjusted death rate between 2010 and 2085. The overall age-sex-adjusted death rate decreases from 2011 to 2086 by 26 percent, 44 percent, and 59 percent for alternatives I, II, and III, respectively.
The average annual death-rate reductions used for this analysis are: 0.39 percent from the alternative I assumptions, 0.77 percent from the alternative II assumptions, and 1.18 percent from the alternative III assumptions. All other assumptions used for this analysis are from alternative II.
